You are not connected to your router

  1. If using a wireless connection, check that the wireless feature on your computer or device is turned on, and make sure the device is not too far away from the router.
  2. If connected with a cable, make sure the cable between your computer or device and the router is securely connected.
  3. If your wireless connection settings (wireless network name or password) have recently changed, reconnect the device using the new settings.

Install the updated firmware

This feature requires newer firmware than what your router is currently using. After you log into your router, select Connectivity, then click on the Check for updates button. Then install the available update.

Can't access Cisco Connect Cloud

Access to Cisco Connect Cloud is unavailable because your current Cisco Connect Cloud session has timed out, or you don't have Internet access. If your session has timed out, simply login again. If you don't have Internet access, you can login to Cisco Connect Cloud offline and check your router settings.
